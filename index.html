Ceci n'est pas un titre

As luck would have it, I've decided to start to blog again. Now before you go and link to this insightful, world-changing post on Reddit, Hacker News and StackOverflow (which you almost certainly won't, anonymous visitor), let me provide a quick disclaimer: the current count now stands at three. Three blogs I've started, and with the certitude of a Trump presidency, have stopped posting on almost immediately after hitting the submit button on the inaugural "Hello, World" post.

Three. Damn. Blogs.

Four if you count Tumblr (which you shouldn't). Five if you count that time in high school when I accidentally published the contents of my entire hard drive to the deep web (oops).
