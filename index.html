I lead the front-end development team at Autovance, where we build kick-ass software tools to help dealerships build trust and sell cars.

I firmly believe that humans deserve better, more humane tools, but in order to do that, the way we think about and build software needs to change.

"Most software today is very much like an Egyptian pyramid with millions of bricks piled on top of each other, with no structural integrity, but just done by brute force and thousands of slaves."

— Alan Kay
