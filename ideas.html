Software Breadboards

An environment for quickly sketching out app ideas/user flows, inspired by the concept of a "software breadboard" from Basecamp's Shape Up methodology: it's definitely not ready for prime time, but I think it's got the bones of a decently expressive rapid prototyping language.

TECHNOLOGIES USED

Angular 12, Peggy.js, Monaco
